Almond Joy is a candy bar manufactured by Hershey's, consisting of whole almonds and sweetened, shredded coconut covered in milk chocolate.

INGREDIENTS:
Corn syrup, milk chocolate (sugar, cocoa butter, chocolate, milk, lactose, milk fat, nonfat milk, soy lecithin, pgpr, emulsifier), coconut, sugar, almonds (roasted in cocoa butter and/or sunflower oil), contains 2% or less of: partially hydrogenated vegetable oil
